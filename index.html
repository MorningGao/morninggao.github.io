Chen Gao

I am currently an assistant research fellow at Advanced Display Technology Innovation Center in Fujian Science & Technology Innovation Laboratory for Optoelectronic Information of China (FJOEL). My group leader is Prof. Enguo Chen at National-Local United Engineering Laboratory of Flat Panel Display Technology in Fuzhou University. Before that, I obtained my PhD degree at College of Optical Science and Engineering in Zhejiang University (ZJU) under the supervision of Prof. Haifeng Li. I got my Bachelor's degree from Tianjin University (TJU). My research focuses on 3D Displays and Augmented Reality/Vtirual Reality Displays.
